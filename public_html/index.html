chthuluscene

Scotiabank Nuit Blanche 2015
Saturday October 3rd
100 McCaul St. Toronto, Canada

Asexual reproduction meets weather pattern. Gesture recognition meets flooded shorelines. Bring your phone. Bring a friend. Pair with a stranger. Change the world. Over and over and over again.
